Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

PagePilot ("the app", "we") is a tool that lets you compose, schedule, and publish video posts to the Facebook Pages and YouTube channel that you manage. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. We keep it minimal on purpose.

Information we handle

  • Account identity. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, used to identify your account and control access.
  • Connection tokens. To publish on your behalf, the app uses access tokens for the platforms you connect (a Facebook Page access token, and/or a Google/YouTube token). These authorize posting to the accounts you choose.
  • Your settings. The profiles you create (source, destination, caption rules, schedule) and which items have already been posted.
  • Videos. Video files you download or select are processed on your device and uploaded only to the destination you pick.

How we use it

  • To authenticate you and enforce access.
  • To publish or schedule the posts you ask us to, to the Pages/channels you connect.
  • To avoid posting the same video twice to the same destination.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties beyond the platforms you connect for the express purpose of posting your content.

Facebook / Meta data

With your permission, PagePilot uses Meta's Pages API (pages_show_list, pages_read_engagement, pages_manage_posts) to list the Pages you manage and publish posts to the Page you select. It only accesses Pages you administer and only performs actions you initiate. Facebook Page access tokens are stored on your device and are used solely to publish to your Page.

Google / YouTube data

If you connect YouTube, PagePilot uses the youtube.upload scope to upload videos to your own channel, only when you choose to. We do not read your other Google data.

Storage & security

  • Settings and platform tokens are stored locally on your device.
  • Your email, device registration, profile settings, and post history may be stored in your own record in Google Firebase to sync across your devices. Access tokens are not synced. Your record is readable only by your signed-in account.

Data retention & deletion

You can remove your data at any time by deleting your profiles, signing out, revoking the app's access in your Facebook / Google account settings, and uninstalling the app. To request deletion of any record we hold for you, email us at the address below.

Children

PagePilot is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended for their use.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
